# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 31, 2025**

## Overview

This privacy policy applies to the 2048 game application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles information.

## Data Collection

**The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or user information.**

## Local Data Storage

The App stores game-related data locally on your device only, including:

- Game progress and current board state
- Game statistics (high scores, games played, win/loss records)
- Game settings and preferences
- Achievement progress

This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is not shared with any third parties
- Is stored only for the App's functionality
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the App

## Internet Connection

The App does not require an internet connection to function and does not communicate with any external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please file a github issue in the current repository.

## Your Rights

Since the App does not collect any data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All game data stored locally can be removed by uninstalling the App.

## Consent

By using the App, you consent to this privacy policy.
